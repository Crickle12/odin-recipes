Jambalaya

This meal is a crowd pleaser. It won first place at our local town's Iron Chef contest. More importantly, it is beautiful over a campfire or in a dutch oven on the stove. Even picky eaters love this meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3/4 lb sausage cut up
  • 3/4 lb cooked ham diced
  • 3/4 lb chicken cut up
  • 2 celery stalks sliced
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 medium green pepper chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper/hot sauce
  • 1 1/2 cup uncooked white rice
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes undrained 28 oz
  • 3 green onions sliced
