Hamburger Helper

This is an amazing dish that my children cannot get enough of. Gluten-free and dairy-free as written, it can be adjusted for your tastes/food allergies. One pot, easy, quick, weeknight meal. Kid pleaser and can easily be doubled or tripled for lots of leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 cup dairy-free milk
  • 1 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 1/3 cup gluten free noodles
  • 1 cup Daiya shreds
  • 1-2 tsp of gluten-free Worcestershire sauce

    • Method

    1. Add in ground beef and diced onion, brown.
    2. Add in all spices.
    3. Stir in liquids and noodles.
    4. Bring to gentle simmer, cover and turn heat to low.
    5. Simmer 10-15 minutes or until noodles are tender and liquid is absorbed.
    6. Remove from heat, let rest 5 minutes for liquid to absorb.
    7. Stir in cheese and Worcestershire sauce.
    8. Enjoy!
