Pumpkin Cranberry Muffins

Perfect fall or winter dessert or lunch box snack for your kids. My children love muffins of all kinds!

Ingredients: Makes 24 muffins

  • 2 cups of pureed pumpkin
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 orange, juice and zest
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp of ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp of ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup frozen or fresh cranberries

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 F and line muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together pumpkin, eggs, sugar and oil until smooth.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, salt, spices and combine with a spatula until all is moist.
  4. Fold in the cranberries gently.
  5. Portion batter into 24 muffin cups, distribute equally.
  6. Bake at 350 F for 25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool on wire racks for 10 minutes then remove from muffin tin.
  8. Cool completely and Enjoy!
